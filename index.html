I am an Associate Professor of English at Shizuoka University, Japan. I edit high school English textbooks with Tokyo Shoseki and Japanese-English dictionaries with Taishukan as well. I love good stories (especially Lord of the Rings), occasionally edit and co-write novels with my friend Andrew, and more recently I'm learning to program in C, C++, and Python, mostly by creating games.
